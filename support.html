Common Questions

  • How do I use CRACK ME?
    Tap the fortune cookie on the main screen to “crack” it open and reveal your fortune. Use the reset button to return to the cookie and try again.
  • What if the app doesn’t load ads?
    Ads are provided through Google AdMob. If ads do not appear, check your internet connection or try restarting the app.
  • Will more fortunes be added?
    Yes! We plan to keep adding new and fun fortunes in future updates.
